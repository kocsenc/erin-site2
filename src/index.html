Christmas Letter for Erin McIntosh

My Dearest Erin,

I wanted to write a love letter, but I am a man of the future so I decided to write you a letter like this. I believe that it is possible to portray the same amount of thought as a handwritten one.

Now its Christmas time and I wanted to express my gratitude towards you and our relationship. You are many things, and I could make an infinite list of your qualities. But above all you are True.
True to yourself,
true to me,
true to our relationship and
true to those you love.
That makes me feel that everything we do is pure and real, and its just great.

You know what I like best about us though? Its the fact that we can have fun and to share interests while still being polar opposites on some aspects. Accepting that we are not the same person yet at times, we are - like a mathematical paradox...

Have a blessed time during Christmas, and see you in a few days for Canada.

Your little infinity,

Kocsen Chung
